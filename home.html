The Journey of Rama

A Story of Dharma, Love and Courage

Ayodhya – The Ideal Kingdom

Rama, prince of Ayodhya, was the embodiment of dharma — righteousness lived, not spoken. His rule was not power, but responsibility.

The Exile

Banished for fourteen years, Rama chose duty over throne. With Sita and Lakshmana, he entered the forest — not as victims, but as guardians of virtue.

The Abduction

Ravana, blinded by desire, abducted Sita. Ego challenged dharma. The cosmic balance trembled.

The War of Lanka

Allies gathered. Hanuman leapt oceans. The battle was not merely weapons — it was the destruction of arrogance.
